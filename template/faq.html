General

I see different prices with the same title. Why ?

The same product can be sold by different sellers. Sellers can list their offers against a certain product while keeping the price competitive.

What should I consider before placing a new order?

Before placing a new order, bear in mind common factors such as price, quality, warranty and any other after-sale services provided by the sellers. We want to ensure your shopping experience is as smooth as possible with minimal errors; therefore, we request you to consider all these factors with care.

How do I see product specifications?

Search for the product of your interest and click on it to view the product page. Scroll down the page to find the ‘Specification’ tab or click on the floating “Specification” button to view all the product specifications.

How do I remove items from my cart?

You can view your shopping cart by clicking on the ‘CART ICON’ on the top right corner of your web page. On each item in the cart, you will see the ‘Remove’ label in the bottom right corner of the item. Click to remove it from your cart.

How can I unsubscribe myself from your newsletter?

You have the option to unsubscribe from our newsletter by clicking on the ‘Unsubscribe’ option at the bottom of the email.

Payments

What should I do if my payment is on hold?

For security purposes, we would need a scanned copy of any government issued ID to be sent to payments@ftrina.com

What are the payment options?

You can choose from the following options to pay for your Ftrina orders: Credit / Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, and AMEX) Cash on Delivery Alipay or WeChat Pay

What is Cash on Delivery?

If you do not wish to make an online payment, you can choose to pay in cash when you receive your order. The amount specified during checkout is the exact amount that you will have to pay, considering the price of the product as well as shipping and carrier charges.

When is payment taken?

You will be charged as soon as your order is submitted.

Can multiple payment methods be combined for the same order?

You can use the available wallet balance along with Cash on Delivery or Credit Card if your wallet amount does not match the entire order value.

Why has my payment card been blocked?

There could be several reasons for this. It’s best if you contact your issuing bank to check the status and resolve such issues. We unfortunately would not have any control over this.

Why is my payment denied?

There can be several reasons for a card payment to be denied. For debit cards, the reason is almost always due to insufficient funds. For failed credit card payments, it might be due to outstanding payments or insufficient credit. Kindly contact your issuing bank to find out the reason for any unsuccessful payment attempts.

My Account

How can I create a Ftrina account?

Getting started with us is easy. Click on our Sign Up page. Fill in the required fields - we'll need your name, email address and desired password (minimum length of password is 8 characters). Click ‘Create Account’. That’s it! You're now part of the Ftrina family!

Can I create multiple accounts?

Yes. However, you will have to use a different email address for each account that you create.

How do I change my password?

Visit 'My Profile'. Click on 'Change' next to your password. Once you change your password, you will receive an email confirmation for the same.

Where can I view all my saved addresses?

Under 'My Profile', click on 'Addresses’ to view all your saved addresses.

Order Status

Is there a minimum order value?

The same product can be sold by different sellers with different minimum order.

What is the wish list?

Like an item but want to save it for later? Ftrina provides you with an option to add products to your wish list, which will be viewed from the cart. You can add items to your wish list by clicking on the “Add to Wish list” link on a product description page. You can move items later from your wish list to the cart and vice versa.

Can I change my delivery details after I have placed an order?

Unfortunately, you’re unable to change the delivery address of an order once it’s confirmed.

Shipping

How do I know the status of my order?

Once you’ve placed an order, you’ll be notified as your order progresses. To check the status of your product, log in to your account and click on the 'Orders' tab. You’ll be able to see the status of each product here. Clicking on a product will also give you detailed information about the status of the product.

Sellers do not/cannot ship to my area. Why?

The same product can be sold by different sellers and different sellers ship to different areas.
